Projects

Cubix Worlds Planner

The Cubix Worlds Planner has been developed to allow players to create and save their unique worlds on the website, which can then be implemented in the Cubix Worlds Game.

Cubix Worlds Wiki Bot

The Cubix Worlds Wiki Bot is a Discord bot designed to provide quick and easy access to information about the Cubix Worlds game. With this bot, users can search for and retrieve information about items, blocks, mobs, and other aspects of the game directly within Discord.

NebulaX

This AI for chess games is designed to use advanced algorithms to make intelligent moves, analyze potential outcomes, and make strategic decisions based on the current state of the game. It is a powerful tool for serious chess players looking to improve their skills and compete at a higher level.
